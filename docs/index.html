What is BubbleFubble?

More than a photo bot — it's a daily adventure

📸

Morning Surprise

Wake up to a new cute animal photo every day — koalas, bunnies, puppies, and more. Never the same photo twice.

💬

Chat Like a Friend

Reply in any language and BubbleFubble chats back — playful, silly, and always kind. It remembers your name, your animals, and your birthday.

🎮

Games & Quizzes

Guess the animal before the photo is revealed, answer trivia, collect trading cards, and keep your chat streak going.

📖

Your Own Story

Name every animal you receive and watch them star in an ongoing adventure story that grows with a new chapter each day.

🌍

Learn While Playing

Discover fun animal facts and learn what each animal is called in Japanese, Swahili, Icelandic, and more.

😊

Always Caring

The bot checks in on how you're feeling, adjusts its mood to the time of day, and sends extra cute photos when you're down.

Completely Free to Run

BubbleFubble uses open-source AI models via Groq — no subscriptions, no credit card, no hidden fees. Ever. Paid AI providers are available as optional upgrades, but you absolutely don't need them.
